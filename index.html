About Me

I am a half-Filipino, quarter-Dutch, eighth-Chinese, sixteenth-German, sixteenth-Irish, Canadian-born citizen of the United States. I have worked for BYU-Idaho Online since 2019 and am currently the Curriculum Maintenance Manager servicing BYU-Pathway Worldwide. I have a B.A in Mass Communication from BYU and a M.A. in Instructional Technology and Learning Science from Utah State. I served a mission in Bucharest, Romania before marrying my college sweetheart, Cameron. We live in Rexburg, ID and have four sons and one daughter. In my spare time I like to spend time with family, watch movies and write. If I could share one thing with my family that I have learned in my life, it's that true joy comes from living the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
